Kobe Bryant

The basketball legend who inspired millions

Here's a timeline of Kobe Bryant's life:

  • 1978 - Born in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania
  • 1996 - Drafted into the NBA straight out of high school by the Charlotte Hornets and then traded to the Los Angeles Lakers
  • 1997 - Wins the NBA Slam Dunk Contest as a rookie
  • 2000-2002 - Wins three consecutive NBA Championships with the Los Angeles Lakers
  • 2008 - Wins a gold medal with the U.S. Olympic basketball team in the Beijing Olympics
  • 2010 - Wins his fifth NBA Championship with the Lakers
  • 2016 - Announces retirement from professional basketball after 20 seasons with the Lakers
  • 2018 - Wins an Academy Award for Best Animated Short Film for "Dear Basketball," based on his retirement letter
  • 2020 - Tragically dies in a helicopter crash in Calabasas, California, along with his daughter Gianna and seven others

"Kobe Bryant's legacy transcends basketball, inspiring countless individuals around the world to pursue their passions with dedication and determination."

-- Los Angeles Times
